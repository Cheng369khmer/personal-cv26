1. Strategic Vision and National AI Policy

Cambodia is actively integrating artificial intelligence (AI) into its digital economy as part of a broader national digital transformation agenda. Central to this effort is the National Artificial Intelligence Strategy, spearheaded by the Ministry of Post and Telecommunications (MPTC).

  • Collaborative formulation involving local policymakers and international experts.
  • Alignment with Cambodia Digital Economy and Society Policy Framework 2021вҖ“2035.
  • Nationwide consultations from government, academia, and civil society.

2. AI in Governance and Business Efficiency

AI deployment in Cambodia targets both private sector competitiveness and public sector efficiency:

  • Private sector: Automated processes, data-driven decision-making, and operational optimization.
  • Public sector: Digital service delivery via AI-enhanced platforms reduces bureaucracy and improves transparency.

The Online Business Registration (OBR) system demonstrates tangible digital adoption, with over 42,000 registered enterprises as of late 2024.

3. Workforce and Skills Development

Cambodia's AI-driven transformation necessitates workforce upskilling. Automation in sectors like manufacturing, logistics, and retail is transforming labor demand. There is a strong curricular emphasis on STEM education and soft skills to ensure adaptability in the AI economy.

4. Ethical and Inclusive Deployment

Cambodia emphasizes ethical AI adoption through frameworks informed by UNESCOвҖҷs Readiness Assessment Methodology. This includes assessing legal, societal, and infrastructure readiness to minimize risks from technological displacement, bias, and inequity.

Summary

Cambodia envisions a smart, inclusive, and resilient economy where AI enhances governance transparency, drives private sector innovation, and supports equitable socio-economic development.
